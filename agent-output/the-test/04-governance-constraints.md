**Assignment**: `b1ad1a690a5148ec8707ff17`

**Scope**: Management Group (Tenant Root `2d04cb4c-999b-4e60-a3a7-e8993edc768b`)

**Enforcement Mode**: Default (enabled)

**Effect**: Deny

**Policy Definition**: `27833bcf-5909-4a37-891c-16a3cb06856d`

**Policy Rule**:

with correct lowercase casing. The `ManagedBy` and `Project` tags from our defaults are
supplementary but NOT required by policy.
